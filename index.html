Saturday Supper Club

Our Mission

At Saturday Supper Club, our mission is to connect people over food. We want to provide a place where individuals from different backgrounds and age groups can come together to hone their culinary skills and build new friendships. Our menus rotate each month and feature exciting dishes such as curries, surf-and-turf, and even breakfast for dinner! If you are interested in joining us, complete the interest form at the bottom of this page.

Past Meals

Who doesn't love breakfast for dinner? Two poached eggs on an English muffin and served with homemade Hollondaise sauce.
